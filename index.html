Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use P2P on a mobile device?

Yes, users are able to make transactions and receive payments using their mobile devices.

2. What are some examples of P2P applications?

There are file sharing P2P applications such as BitTorrent, LimeWire, Kazaa, eMule, Gnutella, and Soulseek.

3. What are some P2P payment apps?

Venmo, Cash App, Zelle, PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.

4. Why is my P2P app not connecting?

If you are connected to the internet, make sure the connection is stable.

5. Why has my P2P suddenly stopped working?

Check if your network is working properly.

6. What can you do with a P2P application?

File sharing, transactions, communication.

Read More about P2P Networks

7. What kind of data can you share with a P2P application?

Music, software, documents, images.

8. Is P2P legal?

Yes, P2P is legal as long as the data is not copyrighted.

9. How can I make my P2P safe?

Immediately update software whenever new updates are available. Use strong passwords and encrypt your data. If you are transferring money, double-check that you are sending it to the correct recipient and not a scam link.

Read More

10. How do I stay safe using P2P?

Make sure you are using a secure network. Stick to P2P apps that you are familiar with and know are safe.
